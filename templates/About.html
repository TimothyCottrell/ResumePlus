About Us

Creating Opportunities
to fully express
yourself

Through a team of college students, driven to give users
the best opportunity to illustrate and present their work
to recruiter.

As college students we recognize that by applying to job positions through applications and
multiple interview we understand the flaws and nature of how inefficient the current job
recruitment system is structured. One of the major flaws that is highly noticeable is that job
seekers are unable to actually distinguish themselves from other applicants. In order to solve
this issue we created this software so users are able to present themselves of their true worth
and recruiter can find the best possible candidate.
